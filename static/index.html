Welcome to Crisis Classifier

This web app allows administrators to train text classification models on batches of URLs. Regular users can then create collections of URLs and classify each URL as Crisis or Non-Crisis using the trained models.

  • Admins: Upload a ZIP of URLs, choose a classifier, and give your model a name. Models train asynchronously and appear in your model list.
  • Users: Log in, create collections by choosing a model, upload a ZIP of URLs, and view real-time classification results in your dashboard.

Get started by registering for an account or logging in if you already have one.
